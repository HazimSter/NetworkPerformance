Analysis of Online Meeting Platform

Based on the research, there are four lecture time during class that is (8am-10am), (10am-12am), (2pm-4pm) and (4pm-6pm). It also analyse which platforms performs the best for online meeting in terms of network performance, packet loss, jitter, throughput and bandwidth.

The amount of data moved successfully from one place to another in a given time period.

- Throughput -

Measure of the amount of data that could travel across a network, from a source to a destination, over a given period.

- Bandwidth -

the variation in time delay between when a signal is transmitted and when it's received over a network connection.

- Jitter -

lost packets of data not reaching their destination after being transmitted across a network.
